Education

I am currently in my final semester of the Honours Business Administration (BBA) program. Here are some projects I've completed during my time at Wilfrid Laurier University:

Integrated Case Exercise

ICE is a required project/competition that all third year students in the Wilfrid Laurier Business program must complete. It involves a real company with an issue that randomized groups of students work together to solve. The competition lasts one week, and by the end, all groups submit final reports and present to the Board of Directors. My team's ability to work together to produce good quality analysis and an effective implementation plan led us to the semi-finals, finishing with an 87% final grade.

New Venture

New Venture is a project/competition done in first year of the program required of all business students. This project is also done in randomized groups and lasts all year long. I worked with my group over the course of the whole school year developing our New Venture idea, a food ordering phone application called Tapn'Snack. We handed in multiple reports, from the idea proposal to the final report. We also presented our idea in the form of a pitch at the end of each semester. It was a rewarding experience to learn and collaborate with my group over such a long period of time.
