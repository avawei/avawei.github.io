Self-Introduction

My name is Steven Zhu and my origins lies in the industrial city of Wuhan, China. My family immigrated to Canada in 2003, back when I was in grade 4. I have worked at business consultant for Rogers, IT support for Ontario Hospital Association, and recently, software developer for TD Canada Trust as a part of my co-op program. I currently attend a double degree program at the University of Waterloo and Wilfred Laurier University. Although my main interests lies with programming, I have kept in touch with business events happening around the world. While my focus is on academics, I also enjoy a good match of badminton and other social activites from time to time.

Work hard, live strong, never give in.
